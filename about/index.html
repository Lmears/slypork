About

Slypork Studio was set up in 2023 by Lachlan Mears with the help of some friends and a lifelong desire for a dedicated creative space.
Nestled amongst the beautiful Port Hills of Whakaraup≈ç, the studio overlooks the magnificent Banks Peninsula.

A born and bred lover of music, Lachie has spent the past 10 years learning how to work with digital audio.
So far, this has culminated in three diverse musical projects, the co-founding of a record label, and four years of experience mastering a wide range of music from the Aotearoa underground.

In 2021 Lachie finished their degree in Computer Science & Psychology and has since grown a deep interest in software and UX, working on the development of a number of quality of life and accessibility apps.
